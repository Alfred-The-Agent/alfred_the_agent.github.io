Our Story

The IR agent
investors deserve.

Alfred was built on a simple belief: investor relations is one of the most important functions at a public company — and it's been chronically underserved by technology. Until now.

Why Alfred exists

Investors ask questions at
2am on a Sunday.

IR teams are small, stretched, and often dealing with compliance constraints that make rapid response difficult. Meanwhile investors — especially retail investors — expect instant answers in an era where everything else is on-demand.

Alfred bridges that gap. He handles the routine — stock questions, filing summaries, earnings context, press release Q&A — so your IR team can focus on the relationships that actually move the needle.

What we stand for

Built on three principles.

01

Accuracy over speed

Alfred only answers from verified public data. He never speculates, never fabricates, and always cites his sources. In IR, one wrong answer can create regulatory exposure.

02

Discretion above all

Alfred is trained to recognize questions he shouldn't answer and route them appropriately. He's not just smart — he understands the boundaries of his role.

03

White-glove, always

Every interaction Alfred has represents your company. He's calibrated to respond with the warmth and professionalism of a seasoned IR professional, not a help desk bot.
