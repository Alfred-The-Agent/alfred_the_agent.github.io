What Alfred does

More than a chatbot.
A full IR concierge.

Alfred handles the full spectrum of investor inquiries — from basic stock questions to complex filing analysis — with the discretion of a seasoned IR professional.

💬

Instant Q&A

Answers questions about financials, management, dividends, guidance, and business strategy — instantly, accurately, from your public data.

📰

Press Release Intelligence

Load any press release and Alfred immediately understands it. Investors can ask detailed questions about your latest announcement the moment it drops.

🛡️

Sentiment Detection

Alfred identifies distressed or angry investors and responds with empathy rather than data — protecting your brand while de-escalating tension.

✅

Verified Callbacks

When a question needs a human, Alfred collects verified identity — name, institution, professional email — before routing to your IR team. No more cold contacts.

📁

Filing Summaries

Alfred summarizes 10-K, 10-Q, and 8-K filings in plain English so retail investors can understand what your disclosures actually mean.

📊

Inquiry Analytics

Every question Alfred receives is logged. Monthly reports show what investors are asking, what's trending, and where confusion is concentrated.
